Export Platinum Frame Transition → .webm

Records the razor-line sweep stinger to a transparent .webm at 1920×1080 60fps. Click Record; the stinger plays once while it captures; when it finishes, your browser downloads transition.webm.

OBS setup: Settings → Scene Transitions → + → Stinger, pick transition.webm, and set Transition Point ≈ 600 ms (half the ~1200 ms total — the full-cover bloom when OBS swaps scenes).

Want a .mov instead? Browsers can only export .webm, but a quick ffmpeg conversion gives ProRes 4444 .mov with alpha:
ffmpeg -i transition.webm -c:v prores_ks -profile:v 4444 -pix_fmt yuva444p10le transition.mov
