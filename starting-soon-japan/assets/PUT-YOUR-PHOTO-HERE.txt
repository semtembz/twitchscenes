Save your picture in THIS folder named exactly:

    intro-photo.jpg

(That temple photo you sent works great.)

- Any size/resolution is fine — the scene automatically pixelates it to match the
  pixel-art look, so you don't need to edit it first.
- Prefer a portrait (tall) image; the left panel is tall and narrow (884 x 1080).
- Want chunkier or finer pixels? Add  ?pixel=120  (chunkier) or  ?pixel=260  (finer)
  to the file URL in OBS. Default is 180.
- Using a .png instead? Either rename it to intro-photo.jpg, or in OBS add
  ?photo=assets/intro-photo.png to the URL.
